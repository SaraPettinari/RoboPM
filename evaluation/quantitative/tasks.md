**Task 1. Control-flow**
Identify the typical sequence of activities for a mission and describe the main phases of the process.

**Task 2. Performance**
Identify delays, bottlenecks, or unusually long-running activities in the process.

**Task 3. Spatial workspace**
Using the x, y, z positions in the log, determine the approximate spatial area of each activity covered by the robot(s). For multi-robot scenarios, indicate whether the robots operate in the same or in distinct regions.

**Task 4. Battery**
Determine whether battery-related conditions affect mission execution, and identify possible battery-related issue.

**Task 5. Communication**
Determine whether there are delayed, or missing messages between robots, and provide one example.
