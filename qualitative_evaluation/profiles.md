# Participant_2
The participant is a postdoctoral researcher with a background in Computer Science and Engineering.

The participant has a strong foundation in dependability and software engineering.

Their research focuses on resilience in robotics, with particular emphasis on software engineering techniques for robotic systems. This includes work on software architecture, dependable self-adaptation, and field-based testing for real-world validation of ROS-based robots. The participant’s overarching goal is to enable robots that can continue to operate effectively under uncertainty and changing conditions, contributing to the integration of resilient robotic systems into everyday societal and work contexts.
